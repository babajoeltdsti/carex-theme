# Carex Terminal Palettes

## Purpose

These palette notes document the ANSI color direction behind the Carex themes so terminal styling can stay visually aligned with the editor.

## Carex Dark

- Black: #45475a
- Red: #f38ba8
- Green: #a6e3a1
- Yellow: #f9e2af
- Blue: #89b4fa
- Magenta: #cba6f7
- Cyan: #94e2d5
- White: #bac2de

## Carex OLED

- Recommended base background: #050505
- Suggested terminal foreground: #d9def9
- Accent direction: Carex Dark ANSI palette with stronger contrast against black

## Carex Dusk

## Accessibility Variants

- High Contrast Dark: pure black background with uncompromising ANSI separation
- High Contrast Light: white background with high-visibility status colors
- Deuteranopia Friendly: favor blue, cyan, gold, and violet contrast
- Protanopia Friendly: favor cyan, gold, and lilac instead of red-led contrast
